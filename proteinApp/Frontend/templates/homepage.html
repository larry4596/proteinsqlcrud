Protein Sequence

A protein sequence is the linear order of amino acids that form a protein. This sequence determines the structure and biological function of the protein. The sequence of amino acids dictates how the protein folds into a three dimentional structure.

Importance

Protein sequences are critical in understanding cellular processes, disease mechanisms, biotechnology applications, and drug development. This helps in understanding how species are related, how proteines have eveolves overtime and how essential biological functions are conserves accross living organisms.
