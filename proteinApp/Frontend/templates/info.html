About Protein Sequence

A protein sequence is the specific order of amino acids joined together to form a protein. This sequence determines how the protein folds and performs its biological function.

Amino Acids

Amino acids are the basic building blocks of proteins. They combine in different sequences and lengths to form a wide variety of proteins with different functions.

There are twenty standard amino acids, each with unique chemical properties that influence protein structure and behavior.

Protein Sequencing

Protein sequencing is the process of determining the exact order of amino acids in a protein.

Importance of Protein Sequencing

1. Helps identify protein structure and function.

2. Aids in disease diagnosis and drug development.

3. Supports evolutionary studies.
